Resources

Large Language Models

  • ChatGPT (from OpenAI) [Link] is a conversational AI developed by OpenAI, capable of generating human-like text responses. It's widely used for tasks such as drafting content, answering questions, and simulating dialogue.

  • Gemini (from Google) [Link] is an cadvanced AI model designed to understand and generate text, images, and code. It integrates seamlessly with Google's ecosystem to assist in various creative and analytical tasks.

  • Claude (from Anthropic) [Link] is an AI assistant developed by Anthropic, emphasizing safety and usability in AI interactions. It's designed to be helpful, honest, and harmless, making it suitable for a range of applications.

  • NotebookLM (from Google) [Link] is a tool that combines note-taking with AI capabilities, allowing users to organize and analyze information efficiently. It's particularly useful for research and study purposes.

AI, Ethics, and Policy

  • The Association for the Advancement of Artifical Intelligence (AAAI) [Link] is a scientific society dedicated to advancing the understanding and responsible use of AI. It organizes conferences, publishes research, and promotes ethical AI practices.

  • The Center for AI and Digital Policy (CAIDP) [Link] focuses on ensuring that AI and digital policies uphold democratic values and human rights. It conducts research and advocacy to guide the development of fair and accountable AI systems.

  • Partnership on AI [Link] This multi-stakeholder organization brings together academia, industry, and civil society to address AI's societal impacts. It works on projects related to fairness, transparency, and the future of work in the AI era.

  • AI Now Institute [Link] is an interdisciplinary research center studying the social implications of artificial intelligence. It provides insights into how AI affects labor, justice, and public accountability.

  • AI Incident Database [Link] This database collects and categorizes real-world instances where AI systems have caused harm or failed. It's a resource for learning from past incidents to improve AI safety and reliability.

AI Image and Video Generation

  • DALL-E (by OpenAI) [Link] is an AI model by OpenAI that generates images from textual descriptions, enabling users to create visuals from prompts. It's a powerful tool for artists and designers seeking creative inspiration.

  • Google DeepMind [Link] DeepMind, a subsidiary of Alphabet, develops AI technologies that can generate and manipulate images and videos. Its research contributes to advancements in visual understanding and creativity.

  • Midjourney [Link] Midjourney is an independent research lab exploring new mediums of thought and expanding the imaginative powers of the human species. Their AI tool allows users to create high-quality images from textual prompts.

  • Adobe Firefly [Link] Adobe Firefly is a suite of generative AI tools integrated into Adobe's creative software, enabling users to generate images and effects using natural language prompts. It's designed to enhance creative workflows.

  • Stable Diffusion [Link] Stable Diffusion is an open-source text-to-image model that allows users to generate detailed images based on textual input. It's widely used for its flexibility and community-driven development.

  • Runway ML [Link] Runway ML offers a platform for creatives to use machine learning models in their projects, including tools for video editing and image generation. It's accessible to users without extensive coding experience.
